ODE X.

Horace invites Phyllis to come and celebrate with him the birth-
day os his patron Maecenas. It is worthy of remark that these
celebrations were always attended with some religious solemnities.
The poet here tells her, that the altars are crowned with vervain,
and waited to be sprinkled with the blood of a lamb. .
